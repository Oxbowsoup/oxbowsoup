About

My name is Caroline, I live in Calgary. I certainly never thought I would end up starting a soup business after being a geologist for quite a few years, but here I am, cooking away! Nutrition and good health have always been important to me and I have always hoped to be involved in the food industry at some point in my life.

I love travelling and tasting food from around the world and you will find bits and pieces of travel in my soups.

What does Oxbow mean? A geological term describing a crescent-shaped lake that forms when a meandering or wandering river changes its course.
